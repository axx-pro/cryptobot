Frequently Asked Questions

Can I try CryptoTrader.Tax before paying?

Yes! CryptoTrader.Tax lets you import your trading history and crypto income for free. You only have to pay when you want to view and download your full report.

How are cryptocurrencies taxed?

The gains, losses, and income generated from your crypto investment activity needs to be reported on your taxes in your home fiat currency (e.g. USD). CryptoTrader.Tax handles this reporting automatically.

How does CryptoTrader.Tax pricing work?

We offer tiered pricing depending on the number of trades per tax season. This means once you purchase a report, you get unlimited revisions for that year.

What is the refund policy?

We offer a full money-back guarantee for all purchases. If you are not satisfied with the report, you can get your money back. You are eligible for a full reimbursement within 14 calendar days of your purchase.

Can I export my reports?

Yes! CryptoTrader.Tax allows you to export and download all of your trading data and tax reports, including the IRS Form 8949.

How do I pay for CryptoTrader.Tax?

You can purchase your report with your Visa, MasterCard, or American Express credit card. SSL encryption and Level 1 PCI compliance ensures that your data stays secure.
