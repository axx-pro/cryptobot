How CryptoTrader.Tax Works

4 Easy Steps To Prepare Your
Cryptocurrency Taxes

Step 1

Import Your Crypto Trades

Start by importing your crypto trading history from all years and from all exchanges into the app. CryptoTrader.Tax will automatically establish cost basis and fair market value for these transactions using historical data.

Get Started for Free
Step 2

Add Crypto Income

Add any historical crypto income such as mining, gifts, or even exchange rewards like airdrops. If none of these apply to you, this step can be skipped.

Get Started For Free
Step 3

Generate Your Tax Report

Once all of your transaction history has been imported, the final step is to review your data and generate your report. You can view a complete example report with the link below.

View a Full Report
Step 4

File Your Crypto Taxes

After your report is generated, you can simply import it into your favorite tax software such as TurboTax or TaxAct to file yourself or send it off to your accountant to handle for you.
